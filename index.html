Once upon a time in Hollywood

My favorite movie is Once Upon a Time in Hollywood by Quentin Tarantino, it features Brad Pitt and Leonardo DiCaprio. They play the role of an actor and stuntman duo. The story is about their friendship and how rapid the changes in the movie industry happened.
